Kimi Cook

Kimi was my first artist, basically my legacy artist She is a wonderful artist, and I fully support her and her goals to be a published author.

Right now, she's been working on her animation, and she does Animation, Art, and fan-made Storyboards for the Warriors series of books.

You can support her here!

Kotirikkin

Kotirikkin is one of my favorite artists that have drawn for me, he feels like a penpal to me at heart.

Our friendship started with a giveaway that turned into a full on artist job. At first it was for free, but I felt guilty requesting so many stickers that I ended up tipping him but today he's become one of my favorite artists no matter the cost he's willing to do it and I really appreciate all his hard work.

He feels like a penpal because, he's from New Zealand. I've never really had a foreign artist before, so this was something new to me. Conversion of Time, and Currency was definately a struggle at first, but it's grown to be quite simple!

Thanks so much Kotirikkin, your art really means alot to me. Keep up the amazing work, and I bet some day you could turn this hobby into something profitable!
